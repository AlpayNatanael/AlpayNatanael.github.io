On the Mittag Leffler Bargmann (MLB) transform

Authors: Natanael Alpay and Kamal Diki

Abstract

The present talk presents a joint work with Kamal Diki, where we introduce the Segal-Bargmann transform associated to the Mittag Leffler Fock space and study how it will be connected to the Fourier transform. We will then also discuss the counterpart of the creation and annihilation operators in this setting. Finally, we also give an extension of the results to case of quaternions, in particular in the slice hyperholomorphic setting.
